ZCOER Pune Highlights 2025

Accordion Icon V3ZCOER was established in 2007. ZCOER is an affiliate with SPPU and is approved by AICTE and Government of Maharashtra. NAAC has accredited the institute with a grade 'A'. ZCOER Pune has signed numerous MoUs nationally and internationally. ZCOER Pune offers full-time engineering courses, including a BTech/ BE in 15 specialisations and an MTech in 5 specialisations. Admission to the BE/ BTech programme of ZCOER is based on the JEE Main and MHT CET scores. For MTech admissions, the college considers the GATE score. The ZCOER Pune annual fees for BTech and MTech courses are INR 1.8 lakh to INR 3.5 lakh., respectively. The last round closing score for BTech ranged between 88.1 and 17.6 as per ZCOER Pune cutoff. BTech in Computer Engineering was the most competitive specialisation. The highest and average package was INR 12.50 LPA and INR 4.50 LPA, respectively during ZCOER Pune placements 2024. The institute has a library, auditorium, seminar or conference halls, guest houses, internet & Wi-Fi, hostels, and canteen, among others. Check below major highlights of the ZCOER Pune.
