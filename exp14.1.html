What students say about Zeal College of Engineering and Research

1.Rutuja Sachin Nikam


B.Tech in Computer Engineering - Batch of 2028
Placements:Placements are average. Many companies visit, but the training and placement cell can do better. Some companies visit the campus, computer, IT, AIML, AIDS have good placement scope. The highest package is 12 LPA till now. The lowest can go till 2.5 LPA to 2 LPA.
Infrastructure:Infrastructure is good, have different buildings. Has good ground for sports. The cultural department is good, has a recording room, guitar room, a dance room, a ramp room, etc. Canteen can be better, not so okay. Department-wise buildings and labs are sorted, labs can be better, though. Sometimes PCs are not working.
Faculty: Faculty is good, some are average. Knowledge study is less than what is needed. Practicals are conducted. Labs are provided. The campus has Wi-Fi everywhere. The course is relevant and makes students industry-ready. Exam difficulty is quite simple, passing marks are achievable if studied nicely.

 

2.Rushikesh munde


B.Tech. in Computer Engineering (Regional Language) - Batch of 2028
Basic details about the college at the ground level.
Placements: Placements are average, not too good, but not too bad. For core branches, placements are a little bit lower. While IT and Mechanical have good placements in this college. The average CTC ranges from 4 LPA to 6 LPA
Infrastructure: The infrastructure is genuinely good. All kinds of facilities are available on campus. It includes Wi-Fi, labs, and a gym. However, there is no boys’ hostel on campus. Many private PGs and hostels are available nearby. The college also has a canteen, playground, clubs, and extracurricular activities. There are some rumors about this college on the internet, but overall, it is an average college.
Faculty: The management and facilities are supportive, but sometimes they are not. Some trainers don't have any work experience. Overall, about 60% of faculty are good, while others are not so good. Faculty members do not stay long, with changes every 2 to 6 months.
Other: The boys’ hostel is not available, but bus service is provided.

B.Tech in Computer Engineering - Batch
It has good infrastructure, a huge campus, qualified faculty, well-maintained surroundings, etc.
